Discover Fibe, your gateway to instant personal loans of up to 5 lakhs. Follow these simple steps for a swift and professional loan application:

Begin by installing the Fibe mobile app. Enter your phone number and complete the verification process.

On the home page, find and click the "Complete Application" button to initiate the application process.

In the upcoming pages, input your personal details. You'll then see the maximum eligible loan amount. To proceed, click on the "Provide Bank Statement" button and effortlessly upload your bank statement.

Select the bank where you receive your monthly salary. That's it! You'll receive a message confirming your request status and the approved loan amount.
